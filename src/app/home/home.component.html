Nina Palumbo is a Chicago-based Illustrator, Musician, and full-stack software developer who specializes in Front-End Javascript Development & Digital Mixed Media Illustration.

About

Graduated from The American Academy of Art, with 5+ years design experience. A 2018 graduate of Coding Dojo Chicago, her future prospects involve using her creative sensibilities to transition in software development and she has since had several contracts. She is a represented by RAW Chicago and previously worked for Reverb.com. Her active band includes Indisposed, which reflects the meticulous and emotional method exemplified in her artistic work. She instructs guitar privately. Once Reverblessons.com highest rated Chicago Instructor, she now books with TakeLessons and has earned over 20 five star reviews as an instructor.
